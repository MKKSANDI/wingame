═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  KNOWN LIMITATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

• Must run as Administrator (by design)
• Some features require restart (Hyper-V, VM Platform, All Cores)
• Cannot detect all GPU models (falls back to "Unknown")
• Windows Home edition: Hyper-V toggle may not work
• Older CPUs: Some optimizations less effective
• Antivirus: May flag registry modifications (false positive)
